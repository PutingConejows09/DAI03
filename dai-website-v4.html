Our History

How We Got Here

2007

DAI Was Founded

Doña Alejandra, Inc. was established in Quezon City with a bold vision to deliver innovative ICT solutions grounded in transparency and good governance.

2010 – 2015

Service Expansion

DAI expanded its portfolio into cybersecurity, cloud hosting, and hardware distribution — growing a diverse clientele across government and private sectors.

2016 – 2020

Global Certifications

Established internationally certified partnerships recognized by CCPIT and CCOIC, solidifying DAI's credibility on the world stage.

2021 – Present

Continued Innovation

Launched CloudPH and expanded into LED walls, interactive kiosks, smart boards, and biometric systems — setting new benchmarks in Philippine ICT.
